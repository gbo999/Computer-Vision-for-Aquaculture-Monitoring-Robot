Key Results: Validated Accuracy

Accuracy vs. Manual Measurements (MAE)

System's carapace MAE (~3mm) comparable to manual variability.

Accuracy vs. Known Physical Size (MARE)

Relative error (MARE) ranged from 3.3% to 6.4% using physical exuviae.

Error Decomposition: Pixel vs. Scale

Over 78% of significant errors ($>5\%$ MARE) were due to Pixel Error (keypoint placement noise).

Scale Error (trigonometric conversion) was a minor factor, confirming robust measurement methodology.
